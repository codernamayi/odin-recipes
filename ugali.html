Ugali (African Cake) for 2

Ugali indgredients

  • Maize Flour 1 litre cup
  • Water 1 Litre
Procedure
  1. Boil your water inside a pot till boiling point
  2. Add your flour and start stiring until you notice the water is gone and the mixture is somehow softly rigid
  3. Keep stiring the mixture until you notice their is an aroma coming out it might take 10mins depending on your heat magnituted
  4. You will notice that the pot's base has a layer and it's turning brown in color, This shows that you are ready
  5. Take Out your Ugali its ready for serving

The Ugali(African Cake) may be served with meat, kale, cabbage and beans depending on your taste
