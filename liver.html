Liver for 2

Indgredients

  • Liver half a Kilogram
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • Oil 3 tablespoons
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
Procedure
  1. Slice up your onions and tomatoes
  2. Have your pot on fire and start with the oil
  3. Start with onions and stir them until they are golden brown
  4. Add tomatoes and continue until they become fleshy
  5. Add a teaspoon of salt
  6. Add your liver and some reasonable amount of water about quatre litre
  7. Cover your pot with a lid and leave them to boil for around 15 minutes
  8. If you are smelling an aroma you are ready to go
Liver can be served with rice, ugali and rotti.
