African Nightshade for 2

Indgredients

  • African Nightshade half a Kilogram
  • 1 Onions
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • Cooking Oil 3 teaspoons
  • Teaspoon of salt
Procedure
  1. Start by picking the leaves from the branches or the whole branch if its for reasonable size - that's for if your retailer didn't do this as an after-service.
  2. Cut the leaves of your african nightshade into big pieces or leave them like that - its up to you if you want strings in your plate you can skip
  3. Wash your african nightshade thoroughly since they usualy come with lots of dirt
  4. Slice up your onions and tomatoes
  5. Have your pot on fire and start with the oil
  6. Start with onions and stir them until they are golden brown
  7. Add tomatoes and continue until they become fleshy
  8. Add a teaspoon of salt
  9. Continue stiring and finaly add your african nightshade in small chucks, they will shrink with time
  10. Add some water to your pot and give it time to boil, about 10 mins depending on your heat magnitude
  11. You should be noticing the aroma, you are ready for serving
African nightshade can be served with Ugali, rice and roti.
